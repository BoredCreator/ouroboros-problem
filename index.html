Welcome

I’m Neel Anshu, and this site is my project for ENGL C1001: Critical Thinking & Writing. It examines a contradiction my own industry is built on: the AI labs that scraped the world’s writing, art, and code to build their models are now fighting to stop imitators from doing the same thing to them. Every page of the argument is a segment of the ouroboros above — and the Works Cited sits at the tail, right where the snake is swallowing it.
