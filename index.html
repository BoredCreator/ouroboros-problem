Welcome

I’m Neel Anshu, and this site is my project for ENGL C1001: Critical Thinking & Writing. It examines a contradiction my own industry is built on: the AI labs that scraped the world’s writing, art, and code to build their models are now fighting to stop imitators from doing the same thing to them. Use the toolbar above to jump to any page, or start with the dilemma itself and follow the loop around.
